TOP 5 most watched Indian film trailers on Youtube

1.Salaar Part 1 Ceasefire-113.2 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 7000 crore 😀

Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire is a 2023          Indian Telugu-language epic neo-noir action thriller film directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under Hombale Films. The film stars Prabhas in the titular role, alongside an ensemble cast of Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan, Jagapathi Babu, Bobby Simha, Sriya Reddy, Ramachandra Raju, John Vijay, Easwari Rao, Tinnu Anand, Devaraj, Brahmaji and Mime Gopi. Set in the fictional dystopian city-state of Khansaar, where monarchy still exists, the film follows the friendship between Deva (Prabhas), the exiled prince of Khansaar, and Varadha (Prithviraj Sukumaran), the current prince of Khansaar. When a coup d'état is planned by his father's ministers and his relatives, Varadha enlists Deva's help to become Khansaar's undisputed ruler.

2.KGF Chapter 2-106.5 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 1200 crore 😃

KGF: Chapter 1 is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language period action film written and directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under Hombale Films. It stars Yash in the lead role as Raja Krishnappa Bairya, also known as Rocky, alongside Srinidhi Shetty, Anant Nag, Ramachandra Raju, Achyuth Kumar, Malavika Avinash, and others. The film is set in the 1970s and follows Rocky, who rises from poverty in Bombay to become a powerful gangster. Driven by a promise to his dying mother that he would achieve wealth and power, Rocky’s journey leads him to the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF), where he confronts ruthless gold mafia bosses and seeks to establish his dominance. The movie blends action, emotion, and drama, showcasing his struggles, ambition, and rise to power in the violent world of the gold mines.

3.Baahubali part 2-183 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 1810 crore 😃

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017) is an epic Telugu film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. It is the sequel to Baahubali: The Beginning and continues the story of the Mahishmati kingdom. The movie reveals the famous mystery of why Katappa killed Baahubali and shows how his son Mahendra rises to take back the throne. Starring Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, and Tamannaah, the film is known for its strong emotions, grand visuals, and powerful action scenes. It became one of the highest-grossing Indian movies and gave Telugu cinema worldwide recognition.

4.RRR Chapter 2-111.8 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 1387 crore 😃

RRR (2022) is a Telugu epic action-drama film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. Set in the 1920s during British rule, the movie is a fictional tale inspired by the lives of two real freedom fighters, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem. The story explores their friendship, struggles, and fight against colonial oppression, combining patriotism with high-octane action and emotional drama. Starring Ram Charan, Jr. NTR, Alia Bhatt, and Ajay Devgn, RRR is praised for its powerful performances, breathtaking visuals, and unforgettable music. It became one of the highest-grossing Indian films and gained international recognition, even winning an Oscar for the song “Naatu Naatu.”

5.Kalki 2898 AD -98.5 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 1100 crore 😃

Kalki 2898 AD (2024) is a Telugu science fiction film directed by Nag Ashwin. The story is set in the future, in the year 2898 AD, and mixes Indian mythology with a futuristic world. It is inspired by the idea of Lord Vishnu’s final avatar, Kalki, who appears to destroy evil and bring justice. The film stars Prabhas, Amitabh Bachchan, Deepika Padukone, Kamal Haasan, and Disha Patani. With its grand visuals, advanced VFX, and unique storyline, Kalki 2898 AD became a major blockbuster and showed a new level of creativity in Telugu cinema.
